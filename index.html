Templates are a checklist of steps to help you complete your assignment. Use the form to create one, and it will show up below "Your Templates."

When you create an assignment, it will show up in "Existing Assignments" tab. Fill out the form above to make one (don't worry about the drop-down menu yet).

All the assignments you make will show up here. Do you see that bar under the assignment? That's the progress bar. It will show you how much time has passed from the time you create your assignment to the time when it is due. Every assignment has one.

Pressing the Done button will mark the assignment as completed and remove it from this page.

Next, let's create a template. Click on the button that says "+Add template".

Great! Now you can select the template in the drop-down menu on the assignments page. To see how an assignment will look with a template, create another assignment with your template.

And that's about it. I hope Homework App proves to be useful for you!
